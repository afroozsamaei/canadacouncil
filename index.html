Grants Allocation by the Numbers

Check Out the Data Trends


The Canada Council promises to transform its relationship with artists and arts organizations across the country, and to increase support to artists, collectives and organizations striving for artistic excellence and greater engagement in the arts. It is also committed to measure its impact and demonstrate results to Canadians. This commitment requires more access to data, more transparency and open dialogue.

The following tool allows you to explore the grants allocation at the Canada Council and offers a visual perception that helps you recognize patterns, trends and correlations. Click on the switch data visualization button and explore another way to check out the data trends!
